PMC reference checker is a quick way of finding out how many references in an article are free to access via PubMed Central.

It is currently in beta so may be a bit unstable. Only data ~2900 articles is available for checking.

Feedback on improvements or suggestions would be greatly appreciated. Please get in touch at robertjsullivan.esq[at]gmail.com.
